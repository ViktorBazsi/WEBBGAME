# Prompt a masik AI-nak (ChatGPT/DeepSeek/Kling)

## 1) Stack es logika rovid, pontos osszefoglalas

Project:
- Backend: Node.js + Express
- DB: PostgreSQL + Prisma
- Frontend: Vite + React + Tailwind

Fo entitasok:
- Character, Girlfriend, Stats, Measurements, MeasurementsGrowth, LiftCapacity, LiftGrowth, EnduranceCapacity, EnduranceGrowth
- A Stats tartalmazza a STR/DEX/INT/CHAR/STA szinteket es a currentStamina-t.
- Measurements a BASE mereteket tarolja (height, weight, biceps, chest, quads, calves, back).

### Growth logika (sajat szamitas a backendben)

A jatek nem a tablakbol olvas ki minden szintre elore beirt erteket.
Csak a BASE (szint 1) ertek van eltarolva, es a growth factorok alapjan SZAMOL a backend.

1) Measurements (meretek)
- BASE: Measurements tabla (strLevel = 1, gender = MALE/FEMALE)
- Growth: MeasurementsGrowth tabla (genderenkent)
- Formula: scaled = ceil(base * factor^(strLevel-1))
- Default faktorok (ha nincs growth rekord):
  - weight, biceps, chest, quads, calves, back: 1.10 (10% / szint)
  - height: 1.01 (1% / szint)

2) LiftCapacity (emelheto sulyok)
- BASE: LiftCapacity tabla (strLevel = 1, gender)
- Growth: LiftGrowth tabla (genderenkent)
- Formula (ketszintu):
  - Szint 2-5: 15% / szint
  - Szint 6-tol: 20% / szint
  - scale = 1.15^(min(str-1,4)) * 1.20^(max(0, (str-1)-4))
- Kimenet: 1 tizedesre kerekitve (Math.round(x*10)/10)

3) EnduranceCapacity (allokepesseg)
- BASE: EnduranceCapacity tabla (staLevel = 1, gender)
- Growth: EnduranceGrowth tabla (genderenkent)
- Formula: distanceKm = round(base * factor^(staLevel-1), 1 tizedes)
- Sebesseg logika:
  - A distanceKm erteke azt jelenti: ennyi km 1 ora alatt
  - speedKmh = distanceKm (1 oras alapido)

### Milyen adatok kellenek a seedhez?
- Measurements: csak strLevel=1 base ertekek (MALE/FEMALE)
- LiftCapacity: csak strLevel=1 base ertekek (MALE/FEMALE)
- EnduranceCapacity: csak staLevel=1 base ertekek (MALE/FEMALE)
- Growth tablak: factorok (MeasurementsGrowth, LiftGrowth, EnduranceGrowth)

Feladat a masik AI-nak:
Adj realis base ertekeket (male/female), es realis growth faktorokat. A cel: a skala szepen, realisan none, ne legyen tul gyors.


## 2) Kepek generalasa (promptok)

### Altalanos elvarasok
- Teljes alak, frontalis vagy enyhen 3/4 nezet
- Semleges hatter vagy transzparens (ha lehet PNG alpha), hogy jatekon belul ra lehessen helyezni a location kepre
- Konzisztens kamera, perspektiva, fenyek (hogy a stage-ek kozt egys√©gesnek tunjon)
- Realista, de stilizalt (nem rajzfilm)
- Outfit stage-enkent valtozhat, de legyen logikus a karakter fejlodesehez

### A) Alap (casual) kepek stage-enkent
Celpont: stage-enkent 1 teljes alakos kep, hetkoznapi ruhaban

CHATGPT/KLING prompt sablon:
"Full-body portrait of {gender} game character named {name}, age {age}, {height_cm} cm, {weight_kg} kg, physique: {physique_desc}, measurements: biceps {biceps_cm} cm, chest {chest_cm} cm, quads {quads_cm} cm, calves {calves_cm} cm, back {back_cm} cm. Str level {str_level}. Casual everyday outfit, clean modern style. Neutral background or transparent PNG. Consistent lighting and camera (full body, 3/4 view). High detail, realistic proportions."

### B) Edzos kepek subactivity-hez kotve
Celpont: subactivity-enkent es str-levelenkent 1 kep, ami a konkret gyakorlatot mutatja.

Peldak:
- Biceps curl
- Bench press
- Squat
- Lat pulldown

CHATGPT/KLING prompt sablon:
"Full-body action shot of {gender} character {name}, {height_cm} cm, {weight_kg} kg, Str {str_level}. Performing {subactivity_name} with correct gym equipment. Show believable form based on lift capacity: biceps curl {bicepsCurl_kg} kg, bench press {benchPress_kg} kg, squat {squat_kg} kg, lat pulldown {latPulldown_kg} kg. Athletic outfit. Neutral/transparent background. Consistent camera and lighting with casual images."

### C) Alvas (sleep) kepek
Celpont: egy kep, ahol a karakter az agyban alszik (subactivity = sleep)

CHATGPT/KLING prompt sablon:
"Full-body or 3/4 body scene of {gender} character {name} sleeping in bed, relaxed posture, calm expression. Soft lighting. Neutral/transparent background (if possible). Simple night outfit. Consistent style and proportions with other images."
